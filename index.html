Personal

Nana Ama Adutwumwaah Agyeman is my name. I am the first of four children from a family of six. I am a God-fearing lady,who believes in Family, Fashion and a strong women's advocate so to speak. Very focused, and passionate about the things I believe in. And oops I am a talker, as in I can talk all day. lol

Education

I am a recent graduate from the university of cape Coast, with a bachelor of science degree in Information Technology. I am currently an entrepreneur-in-training at the Meltwater Entrepreneurial school of technology in Ghana, learning Business, Communication skills and Technology which comprises programming, UX/UI and website designing. I was the general-secretary of the computer science and technology students association of the university of cape Coast.

Interests

Having a particular interests is almost always difficult for me because,like any human,i believe in diversity. More often than not, I find myself gravitating towards God,technology,fashion,and art. But just as likely find religion, politics,and others just as interesting. I gauge my interests by how much time I spend on each of them. Which is my personal relationship with God,reading (just about anything),and indoor. Though I do not use all my daily time engaging in monastic activities, but I am determined to mold my life in a way that is pleasing to God. And that is my greatest interest. As mention earlier I love and believe in technology a lot!!! Because it makes me feel smarter,powerful and it has a tremendous impact on how I complete anything I try to achieve,whether professional or personal. I just cannot imagine a life without it. It's like putting myself in my great-grandma's shoes,hahaha a life with no blender,washing machine,google search(because I am very inquisitive), microwave, dishwasher, should I go on? I also love fashion because its a medium for me to express myself,to use clothes creatively,to dream,inspire and hope for a better life
